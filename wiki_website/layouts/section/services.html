Commissioning and Maintenance Services


Setting up your new technologies should be hassle-free and your systems should operate seamlessly, making your operations efficient and effective. Our specialized services include commissioning your operational technologies and their ongoing maintenance.

From initializing your systems to ensuring they function at peak performance, Helgen's experienced technicians are dedicated to providing unmatched support for a wide range of modern logistics technologies. This includes geolocation, radar, lidars, RFID, facilities access technology, and hardware.

Geolocation of Assets


Track and manage your assets with our advanced geolocation technologies, and let us handle the specialized commissioning and maintenance tasks. We ensure that your systems function flawlessly, offering you real-time asset tracking and management.

Radar Technologies


Our radar technologies offer essential data to drive your logistics operations. From the commissioning phase to regular maintenance, we keep your radar systems calibrated for optimal accuracy and reliability.

Lidar Systems


We provide Lidar systems that deliver high-resolution, three-dimensional information about the physical environment. Count on Helgen for specialized commissioning and preventive and corrective maintenance of these complex systems, ensuring accurate data delivery at all times.

RFID


RFID technology is fundamental in logistics for instant, automatic asset identification and tracking. From commissioning the smallest tags to maintaining the most extensive readers, our team ensures your RFID systems provide accurate and reliable data consistently.

Facilities Access Technology


Secure your facilities with our cutting-edge access technologies. Our team provides comprehensive commissioning services and ongoing maintenance, ensuring robust security and seamless accessibility.

Your hardware is the backbone of your operations. We offer both preventive and corrective maintenance services to keep your hardware in optimal condition, minimizing downtime and maximizing operational efficiency.
